Founder

Dr. Shlomi Sela

Founder & Chief Scientist — PhD in Machine Learning & NLP

RecallVantage was born from a simple question: if consumer complaints contain early signals of dangerous defects, why isn't anyone reading them systematically?

Combining epidemiological surveillance (CUSUM, EWMA) with NLP severity scoring and survival analysis, Dr. Sela built a system that treats automotive complaints the way public health systems treat disease outbreaks — detecting anomalies before they become crises.

The result: a platform that identified every major safety recall in its backtest, with a median lead time of 31 months.
